Our Mission
Our mission is to digitize the voting process of Teachers Association of University of Rajshahi. To cope with the development of the country, teachers association electing is arranged online to simplify, easy and fast the voting process.
Objectives
  • Reviewing the existing voting approach.
  • Coming up with an automated voting system
  • Implementing an automated/ E-voting system
  • Validating the system to ensure that only eligible voters are allowed to vote
